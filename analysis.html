5. How eToro Stands Out

Five differentiation pillars

🎯
Simplicity as a Feature
Nobody else has done this
Perps that feel like buying stocks. Dollar amounts, no order book, holding cost in $/day. The leverage tier (×1 Real → ×2/×5 CFD → ×10 Perp) makes instrument complexity invisible.
👥
Social Trading + Perps
Unfair advantage
Copy perps positions from top traders. Social sentiment ("73% going LONG"). Smart Popular Investor perps portfolios. Nobody has this at eToro's scale (35M+ users).
🛡️
Regulated & Responsible
Trust as a moat
CySEC/FCA/ASIC licensing. Negative balance protection. Segregated funds. ×20 max leverage cap. The mass retail audience that Binance scares away comes to eToro.
💰
Better Than CFDs
Upgrade path for existing users
Current eToro crypto CFDs cost 0.75-2% in spreads. Perps at ~0.10-0.15% all-in is a 5-15× cost reduction. Frame as "same experience, lower costs." Instant adoption driver.
🌍
Multi-Asset Platform
One account for everything
Perps alongside stocks, ETFs, crypto spot — all in one account. No competitor offers this. Binance users can't buy Tesla. Robinhood users can't trade perps. eToro can do both.

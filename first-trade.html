Before Your First Perpetuals Trade
Perpetual Contracts are different from anything else on eToro.
Please read these 5 points carefully:
  1. 1 eToro executes your trade through its omnibus account at Perpetuals.com. You contract with eToro. Losses may exceed your deposit.
  2. 2 There is no expiry date. You must actively manage or close your position.
  3. 3 A Funding Rate set by Perpetuals.com is passed through to you by eToro with no mark-up. This rate can erode your balance even when the market is flat.
  4. 4 If your balance falls below the minimum, your position is closed automatically — no warning.
  5. 5 Auto-Deleveraging (ADL): In extreme conditions, ADL at Perpetuals.com may reduce eToro's omnibus account. eToro will pass this reduction through to your position proportionally. eToro does not absorb ADL losses.

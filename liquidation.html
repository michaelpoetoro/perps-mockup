Reference — Unsolicited Closure Reason Codes
Closure Reason Codes
Every unsolicited closure includes a reason code in the API response and user-facing notification. All are surfaced as trades (not silent deletions) for regulatory compliance.
LIQUIDATION
Full position liquidated
Mark price reached liquidation threshold. Position fully closed. Remaining balance returned.
ADL_EXTREME_VOLATILITY
Auto-deleverage — extreme volatility
Position partially or fully reduced during extreme market conditions to maintain market stability. Can be partial (position remains open at reduced size) or full.
ADL_COUNTERPARTY
Auto-deleverage — counterparty default
Position reduced as part of ADL queue when a counterparty was liquidated and the insurance fund couldn't cover the deficit.
MARKET_CLOSURE
Market/instrument delisted
Instrument was delisted or market suspended. All open positions closed at last available mark price.
COMPLIANCE_ACTION
Regulatory/compliance closure
Position closed due to regulatory requirement or compliance action. Contact support for details.
